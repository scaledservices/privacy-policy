1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Scaled Services ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the personal information of users ("you" or "your") when you interact with our SMS-based games and tools, including but not limited to the Orbit SMS Habit Tracking Game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our SMS-based services and associated web platforms, we may collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Your phone number, and when provided, your name and email address.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our SMS service (e.g., text message responses, check-in data, and progress in the game).
  • Team Data: Information related to team membership, including team leader details and team member progress.
  • Device and Log Data: Standard log data such as the type of device you use and your connection information, collected automatically for system administration purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and manage our SMS-based game and related services, including sending automated SMS reminders, progress updates, and notifications.
  • User Experience Improvement: To monitor and analyze usage patterns in order to improve our services and design new features.
  • Team Management: To facilitate team creation, invitation, and progress tracking, as well as to deliver rewards and milestones as defined by team administrators.
  • Security and Compliance: To ensure the security and integrity of our services and to comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Sharing of Information

  • No Third-Party Marketing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us deliver our services (for example, SMS gateways or hosting providers). These providers are contractually obligated to safeguard your information.
  • Legal Requirements: In the event required by law or in response to a legal request, we may disclose your information to the appropriate authorities.

5. Data Security and Retention

  • Security Measures: We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
  • Data Retention: We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
